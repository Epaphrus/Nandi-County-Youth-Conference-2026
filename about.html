About the Conference

Understanding the vision, purpose, and divine call behind NCYC 2026.

Our Vision

A County on Fire for God

The Nandi County Youth Conference 2026 is born out of a deep conviction that the youth of Nandi County are not an afterthought — they are the frontline of revival in this generation.

We believe God is calling young people from every sub-county, every church, and every background to come together as one — to seek His face, receive fresh fire, and go back transformed.

"Arise, shine; for your light has come! And the glory of the Lord is risen upon you." — Isaiah 60:1
Revival The central heartbeat
Prayer The foundation
Unity One county, one voice
Holiness The standard we pursue
Our Purpose

Why This Conference Exists

Every element of NCYC 2026 is intentional — built to produce lasting spiritual transformation.

01

Encounter with God

Create a sacred space where youth move beyond head knowledge into a living, transforming encounter with the Living God.

02

Holiness & Consecration

Call the youth to a life of purity, holiness, and total consecration — set apart for God's purposes in this generation.

03

Mobilization

Rally and unite youth from all six sub-counties of Nandi County under one banner of revival and holy purpose.

04

Equipping & Discipleship

Equip the youth with spiritual tools — prayer, the Word, worship — to sustain revival long after the conference ends.

05

Community Impact

Commission young people to return to their communities, churches, and families as carriers of revival fire.

06

Spirit-Led Worship

Cultivate a culture of deep, reverent, Spirit-led worship that ushers in the tangible presence of God.

Conference Objectives

What We Are Believing For

By the end of NCYC 2026, we expect to see tangible fruit in the lives of every attendee and across Nandi County.

  • Salvations and rededications to Christ
  • Youths filled and refreshed with the Holy Spirit
  • Healing and deliverance in Jesus' name
  • Strengthened local church youth ministries
  • Deeper culture of prayer and fasting
  • Networks of youth leaders across sub-counties
  • Revival testimonies spreading beyond Nandi

A Word to You

A Message to the Youth of Nandi County

To every young person reading this — you are not too broken, too young, too far, or too ordinary for God to use. This conference is not a programme; it is a divine appointment.

God has not abandoned your generation. He is actively seeking young men and women in Nandi County who are willing to say "Here I am, Lord — use me." The fire of revival is not reserved for history; it is yours, today.

Come with an open heart. Come hungry. Come expecting. What God will do in those six days in Kapsabet will mark you for life.

This is your moment. Do not miss it.

Organized Under

Ministry of Repentance and Holiness

The Nandi County Youth Conference 2026 is organized under the Ministry of Repentance and Holiness — a ministry committed to calling this generation back to God through the message of repentance, holiness, and revival.

The ministry's focus is simple but powerful: present Christ as the only answer for this generation, and equip the Church to walk in the fullness of the Holy Spirit.
