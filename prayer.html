The Foundation

Prayer Is the Engine of Revival

Every great revival in history was preceded by extraordinary prayer. NCYC 2026 will not be built on logistics or programme alone — it will be built on intercession.

We are calling every youth, every church leader, and every intercessor across Nandi County to pray intentionally in the months leading up to the conference.

"If my people, who are called by my name, will humble themselves and pray… then I will hear from heaven and will forgive their sin and will heal their land." — 2 Chronicles 7:14
Intercession Focus

Prayer Points

Use these points as guides in your personal and corporate prayer for NCYC 2026.

1

The Youth of Nandi County

Pray for a great harvest of souls among the youth. That hardened hearts would be broken and many would turn to CHRIST JESUS at this conference.

2

The Speakers and Ministers

Pray for fresh anointing, divine utterance, and the protection of every minister who will speak at NCYC 2026.

3

The Organizing Team

Pray for wisdom, unity, and supernatural grace upon every person serving on the conference organizing team.

4

Spiritual Atmosphere

Pray that GOD would prepare the spiritual atmosphere over Kapsabet — that every hindrance would be removed and the Holy Spirit would move freely.

5

Logistics and Provision

Pray for supernatural provision — for venue, transport, accommodation, and every practical need to be met by GOD.

6

Lasting Fruit

Pray that the fire lit at this conference would not die when attendees go home — that revival would continue in every church, home, and community.

7

Protection

Pray for divine protection over all attendees traveling from across Nandi County — safe arrival and safe return for every person.

8

Holy Spirit Outpouring

Pray for a fresh outpouring of the Holy Spirit — signs, wonders, healings, and powerful encounters with GOD throughout all six days.

Consecration

Weekly Fasting Schedule

We invite all believers across Nandi County to join in fasting prayer leading up to the conference.

Every Wednesday
Dawn to 3:00 PM Fast

Fast from sunrise until 3:00 PM. Spend noon in prayer using the prayer points above.

First Friday of the Month
Full Day Corporate Fast

A full day fast with corporate prayer at your local church or in your home. Pray for the youth of Nandi County.

Week of the Conference
3-Day Pre-Conference Fast

7–9 August 2026. Three days of fasting and prayer immediately before the conference begins — preparing your heart for encounter.

Fasting should be a personal act of consecration before GOD. Those with medical conditions should adapt as needed and seek pastoral guidance.
