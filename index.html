About The Conference

A Divine Gathering
for Nandi Youth

The Nandi County Youth Conference 2026 is a powerful gathering of young believers across Nandi County — a sacred space for revival, holiness, prayer, and encounter with God.

"We believe the youth of Nandi County are called to carry the fire of revival across this nation."

Organized under the Ministry of Repentance and Holiness, this conference is a clarion call to the next generation — to arise in faith, walk in holiness, and impact their communities.
